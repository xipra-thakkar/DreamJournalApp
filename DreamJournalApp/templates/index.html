Manifest In Your Dreams!

Setting Intentions

Before sleep, take a moment to set a clear intention or desire for what you wish to experience or explore in your dreams. Record your dreams in your dream journal immediately upon waking.

Visualization

Imagine yourself within your dream scenario, feeling the emotions associated with achieving your desired outcome. Visualize the details vividly and with positivity.

Reflection

Lucid dreaming, where you become aware of and can control your dreams, is closely related to manifesting. Reflect on the emotions, experiences, and outcomes of your manifested dreams in your journal.
